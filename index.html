Why do ya wanna bother? What's different from the other photography pages?

Aesthetisia, showcases a curated collection of artistic images, all captured using iPhone models from the 4s to the 15 Pro. As a professional photographer since the age of 16, I’ve had the privilege of photographing a diverse range of events, including weddings, get-togethers, and cultural celebrations. Additionally, I am a cinematographer, having worked on the YouTube series “Split”.
